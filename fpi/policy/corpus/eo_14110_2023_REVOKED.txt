[SECTION: DUAL_USE_REPORTING]
Imposed recordkeeping and reporting requirements (under the Defense Production Act) on developers of "dual-use foundation models" and owners of large-scale computing clusters, including reporting of red-team safety test results and information on model training, and physical/cybersecurity measures. This reporting regime was among the actions Section 5(a) of the superseding EO 14179 directed agencies to review for suspension or rescission.

[SECTION: NIST_GENAI_GUIDANCE]
Directed NIST to develop a companion resource to the AI Risk Management Framework specifically for generative AI, a companion to the Secure Software Development Framework for generative AI and dual-use foundation models, and guidelines for red-team testing of dual-use foundation models focused on cybersecurity and biosecurity risks.

[SECTION: EQUITY_AND_CIVIL_RIGHTS]
Directed agencies to address algorithmic discrimination in federal benefits and law-enforcement contexts, and to issue guidance on AI use in hiring, housing, and lending to prevent unlawful discrimination -- a substantially more prescriptive civil-rights posture than the deregulatory policy statement in EO 14179 Section 2.
